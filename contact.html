Frequently Asked Questions

What shipping options do you offer?

We offer several shipping options to accommodate your needs. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days and is free for orders over $200. We also offer expedited shipping (2-3 business days) for an additional fee, and overnight shipping for orders placed before 12 PM EST.

How do I return or exchange an item?

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team or visit our Returns page. Return shipping is free for US customers.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their country.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer the option to pay in installments through Afterpay and Klarna.

How do I care for my silk garments?

Silk requires special care to maintain its beauty. We recommend hand washing in cold water with a mild detergent or dry cleaning. Avoid wringing or twisting the fabric. Lay flat to dry and iron on low heat while the garment is still slightly damp. Always store silk items away from direct sunlight.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer complimentary luxury gift wrapping for all orders. During checkout, simply select the gift wrapping option and include a personalized message if desired. All gift-wrapped orders are shipped in our signature Luxcry Cart box.

How can I contact customer service?

Our customer service team is available via email at support@luxcrycart.com or by phone at +1 (555) 123-4567 during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9AM-7PM EST). You can also use the contact form on this page, and we'll respond within 24 hours.
